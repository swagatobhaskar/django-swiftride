Welcome to SwiftRide.

This page guides you on everything you need to know to use our service.


Opening an account.

You need to have a SwiftRide account to book cars. For first time users, simply Sign Up. After you sign-up, you will be immediately redirected to fillup some more forms. These are:
1. Select a plan.
2. Complete your profile with more details about you.
3. Add your driving license details.
4. Add your debit/credit card for payment thorugh subscription basis.


Billing

As you are required to purchase a plan at the very begining, you are relieved from paying everytime you book a car. The 'one time fee' and 'monthly' fees are deducted based on the duration of your first payment. The car insurance and fuel price are included.


Avail a car

Available cars according to your choise of type and model are shown to you, for the garage you choose. After you book, keep the invoice safe. Visit the garage with the invoice, show it to the SwiftRide employee present there, and s/he will handover the key.


Returning the car

Keep note when you are about to run out of time. We don't yet have the facility to extend your reservation period. Return the car to the same garage.

Cancelling my Reservation

T present we don't have the facility to cancel your reservation. Your money is non-refundable.
